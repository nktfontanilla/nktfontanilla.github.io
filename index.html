What is WriteLite?


WriteLite is one of the newest lighting products designed to solve inadequate
lighting issues when working in a low-light environment. It is a notebook holder
that comes with an LED light that will surely give your eyes the comfort and the
ability to see your work clearly.



Features

Integrated LED Strips

With a thin and flexible set of LEDs that is soldered to a circuit board, the product's lighting comes to life, allowing a more brighter and easier way of taking down notes.

Adjustable Light Brightness

By having a built-in lighting feature, the light brightness can be modified to what best suits your emotions. It can also be turned on or off. It all depends on you!

Long-lasting Power

Instead of using AA or AAA batteries that only lasts for a limited time, WriteLite uses a Zinc-Manganese Dioxide-made 9V battery which will surely last for months.

Sturdy and Lightweight

Who would've think that a foam cardboard can be used as the base of a notebook? Luckily, we did. WriteLite uses this material to make the holder light but well-built.

Portable

WriteLite is a letter-sized notebook holder that can be used in a letter-sized bond paper or any paper with smaller size. With its slick design, it can be brought anytime, anywhere.

Easy-to-use

With WriteLite designed to give comfort to users, it is made simple yet innovative and edge-cutting which will surely give your eyes an element of surprise!
